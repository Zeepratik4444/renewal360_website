How to Run Your Entire CS Operation in Renewal360

You don't need five different tools to manage your customers. Here is how to use Renewal360 as your core CS operating system.

For many teams, "Customer Success" is spread across Salesforce, Zendesk, Slack, and half a dozen spreadsheets. This fragmentation is where churn lives. Renewal360 was designed to consolidate these signals into a single, actionable workspace.

Step 1: Build Your Intelligence Foundation

Running a high-performing CS operation requires a single source of truth. Renewal360 connects to your CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot) and support desk (Zendesk) via secure OAuth.

This creates a **Per-Account Knowledge Layer**. Instead of CSMs hunting for data, the platform automatically surfaces:

  • Usage Trends: Real-time adoption data from your product.
  • Support Sentiment: AI analysis of Zendesk tickets to flag unhappy users.
  • Engagement Recency: When was the last time someone actually talked to this account?
  • Contract Reality: ARR, renewal dates, and expansion opportunities synced directly from your CRM.

Step 2: Design Your Customer Journeys

A great CS operation is proactive, not reactive. Use our **Visual Journey Builder** to map out the entire customer lifecycle. You can create branching paths based on real customer behavior:

  • The Perfect Onboarding: Trigger check-ins at Day 7, 14, and 30 to ensure successful activation.
  • The Adoption Guard: Automatically alert CSMs in Slack if a customer's usage drops by more than 20%.
  • The Renewal Engine: Launch T-90, T-60, and T-30 sequences that draft personalized AI outreach for your review.
Visual Strategy:

Our 2D canvas makes it easy to see exactly how your customers move through your success playbooks. No complex code, just logical branching based on customer health.

Step 3: Direct Feedback via the Survey Engine

Don't just guess how your customers feel. Renewal360 includes a **Native Survey Engine** for NPS and CSAT collection.

  • Automated Triggers: Send surveys after a support ticket is closed or 45 days before a renewal.
  • Health Score Integration: Survey results feed directly back into the account's health score.
  • Closing the Loop: Low scores automatically trigger an "Escalation" task for the CSM.

Step 4: Execute with Human-in-the-Loop AI

The biggest bottleneck in any CS operation is manual writing. Renewal360 solves this with AI that drafts your outreach while keeping you in control.

Every email lands in your **Approval Queue**. You can approve, edit, or reject the AI's work in seconds. This allows a single CSM to manage 5x more accounts without losing the personal touch that prevents churn.

Reply-Aware Automation

The moment a customer responds to any sequence, Renewal360 stops the automation and notifies you in Slack. Your team only spends time on active conversations that need their expertise.

Step 5: Run Your Syncs from the Executive Dashboard

Stop spending hours preparing for weekly syncs. Use our **Executive Dashboard** to see:

  • ARR-at-Risk: The real dollar value tied to upcoming renewals.
  • Renewal Pipeline: A clear 12-month forecast of your retention revenue.
  • Team Performance: Track approval rates, response times, and saved ARR per CSM.

By moving your entire operation into Renewal360, you replace "hoping for the best" with a repeatable, scalable engine for customer success.
