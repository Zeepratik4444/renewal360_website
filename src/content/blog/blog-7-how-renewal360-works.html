The core idea:

A renewal process succeeds when it is hard to ignore, easy to approve, and difficult to forget. Renewal360 is designed as a comprehensive Customer Success Operating System, not just a notification tool.

The Renewal360 Operating System

Renewal360 is an end-to-end CS platform that moves every account through a disciplined success workflow. Here is the architecture of how we protect your ARR.

Step 1

Bi-directional CRM & Support Integration

Start by connecting Salesforce, HubSpot, Zendesk, Slack, and your email workflow via secure OAuth. Unlike basic tools, Renewal360 doesn't just "read" your data—it syncs account owners, support ticket sentiment, and renewal milestones. Your CRM remains the source of truth, while Renewal360 provides the action layer.

Step 2

The Multi-Factor Health Engine

Renewal360 monitors every account across five independent risk factors. The platform adaptively weights these factors based on the contract lifecycle:

  • Product Usage: Deep integration with your app to track adoption.
  • Email Sentiment: AI-powered analysis of communication history.
  • Support Health: Integration with Zendesk to flag Detractors.
  • Engagement Recency: Monitoring champion stability and QBR frequency.
  • Momentum: Tracking explicit renewal commitments as the date nears.
Step 3

Visual Journey Builder & AI Drafting

Using our 2D Journey Builder, you can create custom customer paths. For each triggered account, our AI drafts personalized outreach using real account context. It mentions specific ROI achievements, support wins, or upcoming milestones—saving your CSMs hours of writing while maintaining a premium human touch.

Step 4

The Human-in-the-Loop Approval Queue

We believe full automation is a risk to your brand. Every AI-drafted email lands in an Approval Queue. CSMs can Approve, Edit, or Escalate with one click. If a customer replies, the platform's "Reply-Aware" logic immediately pauses the sequence and alerts the team in Slack.

Step 5

Executive Intelligence & Analytics

Leadership gets a real-time view of the **Renewal Pipeline**. Track total ARR at risk, weighted health distributions, and team performance metrics. See which CSMs are managing the most risk and identify churn patterns before they hit the bottom line.

Enterprise-Grade from Day 1

Renewal360 is built for teams that need enterprise security without enterprise bloat. The platform includes:

  • SSO & MFA: Standard security features for your entire team.
  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Control who can approve high-value renewals.
  • Native Survey Engine: Trigger NPS and CSAT surveys at key lifecycle milestones.
  • OAuth Security: We never see or store your CRM passwords.

Removing the "Action Gap"

Renewals usually fail in the gaps between tools. Renewal360 connects your CRM, your support desk, and your inbox into a single, approval-first workflow. It turns passive data into proactive revenue protection.
